About Me

I am an 18 year old web developer who strives for innovation and discovering new business solutions. I also enjoy improving on existing modern technology. I am a luminary , a friend, and a hard working employee. Being the youngest in a family of eight I am driven by change, goals, and longshots. I was born and raised in my hometown of Music City, Nashville. Growing up I have always loved video games, computers and the web. I remember the embarrassing day in elementary school when I found out that a the monitor was not the computer. Later on when I graduated high school I built my first P.C. and I fell in love with the process as well as the technical aspect. My love for code was formed later when I started reading ‘A Smarter Way To Learn HTML and CSS’ and ‘A Smarter Way To Learn Javascript’ by Mark Myers. From there I pursued my passion by enrolling into DevMountain’s Web Development Immersive course in March that is located in Dallas, Texas. While in school I won the “Best Business Solution” award out of my entire class for my innovative web application. I proceeded to graduate the following June of 2018, and I am currently working on new ideas and learning new technologies! Feel free to contact me for any inquiries or opportunities!

Wish Trip

An application to take on the load of vacation planning to take the hassle off of an average user. I was mainly in charge of using Puppeteer to web scrape travelocity and get the flights as well as hotels using the dates and locations that the user inputs. This Project allows the user to schedule up to a 28 day vacation and add agendas to each day.
